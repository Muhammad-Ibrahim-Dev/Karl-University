Bachelor of Engineering

The Bachelor of Engineering program at Karl University offers a comprehensive and rigorous education in engineering principles and practices. Students will explore various engineering disciplines, including electrical, mechanical, civil, and more.

Our program emphasizes hands-on learning, with state-of-the-art labs and facilities to provide students with practical experience. Graduates will be well-prepared for successful careers in engineering, construction, project management, and other related fields.

Key Features

  • Experienced faculty with industry expertise
  • Modern engineering labs and equipment
  • Internship opportunities with leading engineering firms
  • Industry partnerships and networking events
  • Opportunities for research and innovation

Curriculum

The curriculum is designed to provide a solid foundation in engineering principles and includes specialized courses in various engineering disciplines, such as:

  • Introduction to Engineering
  • Engineering Mathematics
  • Mechanics and Materials
  • Electrical Circuits
  • Thermodynamics
  • Structural Analysis
  • Fluid Mechanics
  • Control Systems
  • Environmental Engineering

Admission Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA of 3.2
  • Strong background in mathematics and science
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Personal statement

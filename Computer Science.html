Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science program at Karl University offers comprehensive training in computer science and software development. Students will gain a deep understanding of programming, algorithms, data structures, software engineering, and more.

Our program focuses on practical hands-on experience, allowing students to work on real-world projects and develop the skills needed to excel in the tech industry. Graduates will be well-prepared for careers in software development, web development, data analysis, and various other technology-related fields.

Key Features

  • Expert faculty with industry experience
  • State-of-the-art computer labs and facilities
  • Internship opportunities with top tech companies
  • Networking events and career fairs
  • Opportunities for research and innovation

Curriculum

The curriculum includes a broad range of courses to provide a strong foundation in computer science, including:

  • Introduction to Computer Science
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Programming in Python, Java, and C++
  • Web Development
  • Database Systems
  • Software Engineering
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Machine Learning
  • Operating Systems
  • Computer Networks

Admission Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0
  • Strong background in mathematics and science
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Personal statement
